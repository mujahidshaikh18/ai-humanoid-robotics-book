Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics — A Complete Guide

Welcome to the comprehensive educational resource for robotics students learning Physical AI, Humanoid Robotics, ROS 2, Isaac, Gazebo, Unity, VLA, and AI-driven humanoid systems.

Course Modules​

This course is organized into four comprehensive modules:

Module 1: The Robotic Nervous System (ROS 2)​

  • Chapter 1: Introduction to ROS 2 and rclpy
  • Chapter 2: ROS 2 Nodes, Publishers, and Subscribers
  • Chapter 3: Services and Actions in ROS 2
  • Chapter 4: ROS 2 Architecture and Communication Patterns
  • Chapter 5: Advanced ROS 2 Concepts and Best Practices

Module 2: The Digital Twin (Gazebo & Unity)​

  • Chapter 6: Introduction to Digital Twins in Robotics
  • Chapter 7: Gazebo Physics Simulation for Humanoids
  • Chapter 8: Unity Integration for High-Fidelity Simulation
  • Chapter 9: Sensor Simulation and Environment Building
  • Chapter 10: Digital Twin Applications in Robotics

Module 3: The AI-Robot Brain (NVIDIA Isaac)​

  • Chapter 11: Introduction to NVIDIA Isaac Platform
  • Chapter 12: Isaac Sim for Photorealistic Simulation
  • Chapter 13: Isaac ROS for Hardware-Accelerated Robotics
  • Chapter 14: Navigation and Reinforcement Learning
  • Chapter 15: Sim-to-Real Transfer Techniques

Module 4: Vision-Language-Action (VLA)​

  • Chapter 16: Introduction to Vision-Language-Action Systems
  • Chapter 17: Voice-to-Action with Whisper Integration
  • Chapter 18: LLM-Based Cognitive Planning
  • Chapter 19: Multimodal Perception and Sensor Fusion
  • Chapter 20: Capstone Project — Autonomous Humanoid

Getting Started​

Begin with Module 1, Chapter 1 to start your journey into AI-powered humanoid robotics.
